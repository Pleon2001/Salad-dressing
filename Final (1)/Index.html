Nike Pegasus 38

Image by Nike.com



A workhorse of elite athletes and weekend warriors alike, the Nike Pegasus 38 is ubiquitous thanks to its versatility and eye-catching swagger. Nike React foam is lightweight, bouncy and energetic, helping you bound effortlessly through any workout. It handles high mileage gracefully, and it's strong enough to take on cross-training and strength sessions. Last year, the Pegasus’s midsole switched from older Cushlon foam to more-responsive React, and Nike added two more millimeters of it underfoot.

Nike also lowered the pressure in the air unit in the women’s model (15 PSI, compared to 20 PSI for men) to make it a touch softer, doubled the size of the forefoot unit for extra pop on toe off, and scrapped the air unit from the midfoot and heel. The outsole got a facelift, too, with more flex grooves and a rectangular tread pattern that slightly improves grip for short stints offroad.
